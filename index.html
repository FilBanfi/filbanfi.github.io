// who I am

About

Independent developer focused on Android, Linux, and open source projects.

I mainly work with Kotlin and the modern Android stack (Jetpack Compose), but I also build experimental tools in Bash, Python, and C++. I'm interested in building complete systems: from mobile apps to OS-level projects and custom ROMs.

My approach is practical: rapid prototyping, continuous testing, and constant iteration. Some projects are experimental or incomplete by choice, as part of the learning process.

Current focus: Android development, personalized Linux systems, and open source projects (GPLv3).

I build to understand.

If a project matters to me, I finish it — no matter what, until it's perfect.

Shipping matters more than perfection, but quality is non-negotiable.
