This is me

Who I am

Hello! I'm Filippo Banfi, an Italian indie developer and content creator. My goal is to build high-quality, lightweight tools for your devices that are fully privacy-first and open source, while documenting everything on social media so everyone can understand how the tech world works.

Why FilBanfi

FilBanfi was born as a seller of refurbished phones on an Italian website called Subito.it, which is still active and working.

But that's not my first project in the tech world. My first attempt was a blog called Informaticando.data.blog, in Italian, whose name can be translated as "doing IT", hosted on WordPress; it lasted about one year.

After another year, I decided to try again, but not with Informaticando. I wanted to keep it as I left it, so I created a new blog, 404byte.blogspot.com, hosted on Blogger by Google, that unfortunately didn't last long, due to school commitments.

The summer of the same year, I decided to try to fix my old phone, and I really enjoyed the activity, so I decided to fix other spare devices in my house and sell them on the website mentioned at the beginning of this text. That's how the name FilBanfi was born, then I just drew a logo and decided to distribute my apps and projects under this name instead of just publishing them on random WordPress websites.
